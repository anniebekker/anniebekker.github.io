About Me

I started digital drawing in high school in an attempt to capture the details of my high school crush's sweet face. For fun I now use a combination of pencil sketching and Photoshop to draw people I see at ice-cream shops and parties.

I'm 21 years old, living in Cape Town and studying Interactive Media and Film at UCT.
